🌟 Your Personalized 2-Minute Introduction — Handy Heathers-HJ SERVICES LLC

📣 This script is written and ready for you to deliver. Read it aloud 2–3 times before the webinar to build confidence. It runs approximately ~200 words / 90–120 seconds at a comfortable pace — giving you a little breathing room within the 2-minute limit.

0:00 "Good morning, everyone — my name is Heather, and I am the founder and owner of Handy Heathers-HJ SERVICES LLC."

0:10 "We are a woman-owned small business specializing in professional and administrative services as well as IT support. From document management, program coordination, and executive support to technical help-desk and IT solutions, we deliver reliable, detail-oriented services that keep operations running smoothly."

0:40 "Our work is centered on the Department of Defense contracting space. We understand the compliance requirements, security protocols, and performance standards that come with supporting government missions — and we take that responsibility seriously."

1:10 "As a certified woman-owned small business, we bring both the agility of a small firm and the professionalism that DOD prime contractors and program offices require. We are committed to delivering quality, meeting deadlines, and being the kind of partner you can count on."

1:30 "Today, I am here to connect with prime contractors, program managers, and agencies who need a trusted small business partner for administrative support or IT services on DOD contracts. Whether as a subcontractor or teaming partner, we are ready to contribute."

1:50 "If that aligns with what you're looking for, I would love to start a conversation. Thank you — I'm Heather with Handy Heathers-HJ SERVICES LLC."

💡 Delivery Tips
Pace: Speak slowly and clearly — nerves speed us up. Take a breath at each paragraph break.
Eye contact: Look at the camera, not the screen, to appear confident and direct.
Emphasis: Stress "woman-owned," "DOD," and "IT support" — these are your differentiators in this audience.
End strong: Smile when you say your company name at the close — it's warm and memorable.
Personalize if needed: If your breakout room is IT-focused, lead with IT; if it's administrative/professional services, lead with that. Swap the order at the 0:10 mark.

🎤 2-Minute Company Introduction Template

Use the prompts below to build your introduction. Keep it under 2 minutes.

1
Company Name & Location

State your company's name and where you are based.

e.g., "We are [Company Name], headquartered in [City, State]."
2
Core Capabilities / Primary Services or Products

Briefly describe what your company does.

e.g., "We specialize in [services/products], serving [target market or industries]."
3
Experience or Interest Related to This Sector / Program

Share relevant background or why you're attending this event.

e.g., "We have [X years of experience / a strong interest] in [sector/program]."
4
What You Are Seeking from Potential Partners

Be clear about the partnerships or conversations you're looking for.

e.g., "Today we are looking to connect with [type of partners] to [goal or opportunity]."
